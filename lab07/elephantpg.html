Welcome to Elephant Showdown!!!

An ethical elephant wrestling show!

Ever wanted to see animals wrestle without worrying about potential animal abuse or unsafe conditions? Well, now you can! We've anthropomorphized 20 elephants that wanted to enter a wrestling tournament and given them the chance to win $20,000!!! They're big, they're mean, and they are ready to for a SHOWDOWN

Here is the winner of last years Showdown! Abagbe tore through the competion, with each round lasting less than a minute! Fun Fact about Abagbe, because her we had to introduce new rules and regulations to make things more fair for the other competitors!
